INFO:

Kongu Engineering College was set and established in the beautiful scenic district of Erode located in the State of Tamil Nadu.The college started in the year 1984 initially offered only Engineering courses and after 1990 started providing Bachelors in Technology related courses as well. Now the college offers courses in Humanities and Science categories along with Management courses in MBA and MCA.
